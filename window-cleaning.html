Window Cleaning Service

There are multiple ways to do any window cleaning job. The service we provide falls into one of three categories to suit your specific needs.

This is our traditional hand clean with a squeegee applicator and proprietary window cleaning solution. In some cases we may have to scrub and rinse with a water fed pole designed just for window cleaning. These methods are effective at removing dust, dirt, and most bird droppings.

Perfomed like a traditional hand clean but with a specialized window scraper. This method is best suited for removing stuck on grime such as silicone, stubborn bird droppings, construction debris, tree sap and waxed base products. 

A window restoration is needed when the glass is covered in a haze that limits visibilty. This procedure includes a construction clean with restoration paste and polish, or an acid wash to remove hard water minerals that may have formed over time.

Expect amazing results, we'll make it happen.
